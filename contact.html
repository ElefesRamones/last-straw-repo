Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with me

Are you available for freelance work?

Yes! I'm currently open to freelance opportunities in design, video editing, and social media content creation. I work with both individuals and businesses on projects of all sizes.

What tools do you use?

I use industry-standard tools including Adobe Photoshop, Premiere Pro, After Effects, Figma, Canva, and CapCut. I stay updated with the latest software and techniques to deliver high-quality results.

Can you create content for my brand or social media?

Absolutely! I specialize in creating branded visuals and short-form video content for digital platforms. I can help develop a cohesive content strategy that aligns with your brand identity and engages your target audience.

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. Simple graphic designs might take 2-3 days, while comprehensive video editing projects could take 1-2 weeks. I always discuss timelines upfront and provide realistic estimates.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes, I include revisions in all my projects to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final result. The number of revisions depends on the project scope, which we'll discuss during our initial consultation.
