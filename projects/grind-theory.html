Project Overview

Grind Theory is a concept apparel design project that showcases my ability to create compelling shirt designs and develop cohesive brand identity from scratch. This personal project demonstrates my skills in conceptual thinking, visual design, and brand development for a fictional streetwear brand.

The design philosophy centered around motivation and grind culture, creating bold visuals that would appeal to young entrepreneurs and hustlers. Every design element was crafted to inspire and motivate, from individual shirt graphics to promotional advertisements and brand materials.

The Challenge

Creating a distinctive fictional apparel brand from concept to execution, developing a complete visual identity that would demonstrate my design skills across multiple mediums - from shirt graphics to promotional materials and brand assets.

The Solution

Developed a comprehensive brand identity system featuring motivational graphics, consistent typography, and a cohesive color palette. Created multiple shirt designs and promotional materials that showcase my ability to maintain brand consistency while exploring creative visual concepts.
